A Final Word


  • Ginger Soda

    Ginger Soda, also known as Ginger Ale, is a carbonated soft drink flavoured with ginger. On principle it is to be regarded as detestable, detractable, and depressing as a beverage choice.

  • Hand Sanitizer

    Hand Sanitizer, containing at least 75% alcohol, should have a Bacteriostatic Rate of 99.9%. Anything less is worthy of the utmost contempt. Once I read a package that said, "Kills up to 99.9% of most bacteria" As if they're going to slip it past us as the consumer that some Escherichia or Pseudomonas made it past the bouncer because they're cute.

  • Penang Curry

    Penang Curry, see also "phanaeng", is a Thai curry which is sweet and salty with a nutty peanut flavor. The best Penang's are at the mercy of their restuarant under the following condition: they serve an appropriate portion of rice to not exceed 2 cups, the protein is sliced (not diced or shredded), the extra filligree, aka vegetables, are steamed not fried, and the restuarant has a Spice Caddy with a vinegar based spice, fish sauce based spice, chili paste, and chili flakes. Oh my, just thinking of it makes my mouth water. Do more of this.

    Note: not all resturants are the same, there are offenders amongst us who not only lack a Spice Caddy but look at you in confusion when you ask for one. Sriracha is NOT an appropriate substitute...

  • Sales; 1/2 Off

    If you're having a sale and you declare boldy on your sinage, "Buy One Get One Free!" Don't ring up the most expensive item first at 1/2 price, then the second item at 1/2 price. That's "Buy Two Items, The Most Expensive of Which Will be the Basis of the Price, Wherein the Least Expensive of Which Will be the Remainder of the Price, Afterwhich Both Items will be Priced at 50%" This is what I call, "false advertising" and is infuriating.

  • Salutations

    Salutations are a necessary part of interaction. For every beginning there must be an end. You open and you close. Such is the way of life. Therefore, do not leave open ended converstaions in the wake of your social fluttering abouts. Say goodbye, goodnight, au revoir, konbonwa, and auf wiedersehen.
